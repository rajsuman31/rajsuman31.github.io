About


Suman Raj is a 2nd year Ph.D. student in the Department of Computational and Data Sciences. She is working in DREAM:Lab under the guidance of Prof. Yogesh Simmhan. She has completed her undergraduate in Electrical and Electronics Engineering from National Institute of Technology, Tiruchirappalli. She has worked at Publicis Sapient as a Data Engineer.

Research


My research area lies at the intersection of Robotics and Artificial Intelligence. I am primarily working on edge computing, data management and scheduling for autonomous aerial vehicles (UAV) or Drones that explores task scheduling and vehicle routing to assist the visually impaired. I am also working on a federated query engine over time-series data that pushes to the edge from the fog.
